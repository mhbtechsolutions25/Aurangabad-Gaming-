About Us

Aurangabad Gaming is a YouTube channel and community initiative built to encourage and bring up the gaming culture in our city. Our vision is to build the local gaming community and scale up to a national level.

We are on a mission to expand the gaming culture beyond mobile games like BGMI, introducing players to the high-performance world of PC and Console gaming, including titles like Valorant, Minecraft, Mortal Kombat, and GTA.
