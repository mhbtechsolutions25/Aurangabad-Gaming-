About Aurangabad Gaming

Aurangabad Gaming is a channel formed with a vision to strengthen the gaming community in Aurangabad, Maharashtra. Our goal is to expand and unite gamers through competitive and community-driven gameplay across multiple games.

We focus on building a strong gaming ecosystem for popular games like Minecraft, Mortal Kombat, Valorant, BGMI, and more. Through gameplay, events, and community interaction, we aim to grow one of the strongest gaming communities in the region.
